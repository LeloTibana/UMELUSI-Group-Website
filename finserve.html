Transaction Requirements

Eswatini Resident
  • Original ID/Passport (not copies)
  • Proof of residence: A utility bill (water or electricity) or lease agreement, not older than 3 months.
  • Flight ticket (Travelling outside CMA)
  • Source of funds (Travelling outside CMA)
  • Country visa (Buy Transactions)
Non-resident (Permit)
  • Original Valid Passport
  • Original Valid Permit (A4 with photo)
  • Flight ticket (Travelling outside CMA)
  • Source of funds (Travelling outside CMA)
  • Country visa (Buy Transactions)
Visitor
  • Original Passport
  • Immigration visa/permit/stamp

Eswatini Resident
  • Original National ID Card
  • Passport (Include police report if ID is Lost)
  • Proof of residence: A utility bill (water or electricity) or lease agreement, no older than 3 months.
Non-Resident (Permit)
  • Original Valid Passport
  • Original Valid Permit
  • Proof of residence (applicable yearly)
  • Source of funds (Pay slip applicable yearly)
Visitors
  • Original Valid Passport
  • Immigration visa/permit/stamp
  • Proof of residence (Sending & transactions above SZL 5,000)
  • Source of funds (Sending transactions)
