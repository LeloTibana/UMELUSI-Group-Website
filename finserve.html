Foreign Exchange Services

Competitive rates and expert advice with no hidden fees.

  • Major currencies in stock: USD, EUR, GBP, ZAR
  • Cash, travellers cheques, prepaid cards
  • Rate alerts & forward contracts
  • Bulk corporate rates
  • Personalised currency advice
  • No commission charges

Perfect for travel, trade, or hedging.

Personal Identification

  • Valid passport, driver's license or national ID
  • Proof of address (utility bill or bank statement less than 3 months old)
  • For non-residents: valid visa or work permit

Transaction Documentation

  • Source of funds declaration for amounts over certain thresholds
  • Recipient details for Western Union transfers
  • Invoice or purpose statement for business FX transactions

Requirements ensure compliance with anti-money laundering regulations and secure processing.
