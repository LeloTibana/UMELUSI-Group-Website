Client Services Administrator

Remote • Posted: April 29, 2025

Role Overview

As a Client Services Administrator, you'll be the first point of contact for clients and prospects across Umelusi's subsidiaries. You'll ensure a seamless onboarding experience, maintain accurate records, and deliver high-quality support to stakeholders.

Key Responsibilities

  • Manage inbound inquiries via phone, email, and form submissions
  • Coordinate client onboarding and KYC documentation
  • Update CRM records and prepare internal reports
  • Support the Client Relationship team in retention and re-engagement

Minimum Requirements

  • Diploma or certificate in Business Administration or Finance
  • 2+ years in client-facing roles or support environments
  • Strong interpersonal and organizational skills
  • Familiarity with Microsoft Office, Google Workspace, and cloud CRMs
