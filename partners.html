Flagship Case Study: Eswatini Government Salary Review

Project Overview and Execution

Umelusi Partners serves as the technical lead and independent advisor to the Government of Eswatini on a critical full salary review and pay reform programme.

Our mandate covers job evaluation, market benchmarking, grading structure design, fiscal impact modeling, and stakeholder engagement to deliver an equitable, affordable, and sustainable civil service compensation framework.

The expected outcomes include greater internal equity, improved talent retention, transparent pay decisions, and strengthened HR governance aligned with national development goals.
