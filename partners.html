Flagship Case Study: Eswatini Government Salary Review

Showcasing Umelusi Partners' capacity for large-scale, complex public sector transformation.

Project Overview and Execution

Umelusi Partners serves as the **technical lead and independent advisor** to the Government of Eswatini on a critical full salary review and pay reform programme. Our mandate is comprehensive, covering initial job description audits and systematic evaluation to create a consistent role taxonomy. We conduct thorough national and regional market salary **benchmarking** to identify gaps, and subsequently design a transparent grading structure and pay bands. Core activities involve rigorous fiscal impact modeling, producing phased **costing scenarios** to ensure affordability, and facilitating extensive stakeholder engagement workshops with unions, ministries, and finance teams to build crucial consensus and ownership.

The primary **key deliverables** include the final grading and salary structure, a detailed fiscal costing report, a robust job evaluation manual, and a complete implementation roadmap with supporting training materials. The expected **outcomes and benefits** are centered on achieving greater internal equity and role clarity across the civil service, which in turn improves the ability to attract and retain critical skills. Ultimately, this reform leads to transparent, defensible pay decisions that lower industrial risk and establish a strengthened HR system for ongoing pay governance, aligning fiscal prudence with national development goals.
