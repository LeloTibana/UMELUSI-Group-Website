**Strategic Corporate Finance for Growth**

Umelusi Capital is the investment banking and corporate finance arm of Umelusi Group. We deliver comprehensive financial structuring and advisory services designed to accelerate corporate growth and unlock long-term value for our clients across Africa.

We partner with institutional clients, governments, and large corporates on complex transactions, including capital raising, restructuring, mergers, and acquisitions. Our expertise spans equity markets, debt capital markets, and bespoke structured finance solutions.

Explore Our Services

Our Core Focus

**Capital Raising**

Structuring and placement of equity, mezzanine, and debt financing for development and expansion.

**Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A)**

Advisory on divestitures, strategic acquisitions, valuation, and post-merger integration services.

**Structured Finance**

Bespoke financing solutions, including securitization, project finance, and tailored derivatives strategies.

**Corporate Restructuring**

Guidance on balance sheet optimization, financial distress, and effective turnaround strategies.

**Leading Debt & Equity Markets**

Our team provides expert execution across primary and secondary markets, ensuring clients access the optimal mix of capital at competitive terms.

Equity Capital Markets (ECM): IPOs, secondary offerings, and private placements.
Debt Capital Markets (DCM): Corporate bonds, government securities, and syndicated loans.
Securities Trading: Institutional brokerage services and tailored liquidity solutions.
