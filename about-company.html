Company Information

Founded in 1993, Umelusi Group is a diversified financial services company committed to empowering individuals, institutions, and communities through world-class investment and financial advisory services.

With a legacy rooted in trust, excellence, and innovation, we’ve built a solid track record of delivering personalized solutions through our four core subsidiaries: Umelusi Fund Managers, Umelusi Partners, Umelusi Capital, and Umelusi FinServe.

Our mission is simple: Guide. Guard. Grow. We believe in long-term partnerships, value-driven strategies, and financial freedom that goes beyond returns.

What Sets Us Apart?

Over 30 years of industry experience

Assets under management exceeding E1.3 billion

A cross-functional team of experts in finance, law, and risk

Ethical governance and a client-first philosophy

Pan-African perspective with global capabilities

Deep commitment to innovation and long-term value
