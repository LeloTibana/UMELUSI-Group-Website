Empowering Growth Through Capital Excellence

Umelusi Capital provides corporate finance, investment banking, and advisory solutions that help businesses access, manage, and grow capital effectively across markets.

Who We Are

Umelusi Capital is the corporate finance and investment banking arm of Umelusi Group, offering strategic financial solutions to corporations, investors, and institutions.

We specialize in capital raising, mergers and acquisitions (M&A), debt advisory, and market entry structuring. Our mission is to empower African and global businesses with innovative financial strategies that foster sustainable growth and long-term value creation.

Our Pillars

Financial Expertise

Deep industry knowledge and technical capability drive our execution and insights.

Innovation

We apply creative financial engineering to solve complex capital challenges.

Integrity

Our advice is objective and independent — built on transparency and trust.

Strategic Partnership

We partner with clients as long-term advisors to create enduring impact.
