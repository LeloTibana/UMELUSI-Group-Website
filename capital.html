Strategic Corporate Finance for Growth

Umelusi Capital is the investment banking and corporate finance arm of Umelusi Group. We deliver comprehensive financial structuring and advisory services designed to accelerate corporate growth and unlock long-term value for our clients across Africa.

We partner with institutional clients, governments, and large corporates on complex transactions, including capital raising, restructuring, mergers, and acquisitions. Our expertise spans equity markets, debt capital markets, and bespoke structured finance solutions.

Explore Our Services

Our Corporate Finance Expertise

We offer bespoke investment banking services designed to meet the complex financial needs of mid-market enterprises across the African continent.

Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A)

Strategic advisory for buyers and sellers, covering divestitures, hostile takeovers, and cross-border transactions.

Capital Raising

Private Equity placement, Venture Capital funding, and IPO advisory to secure the optimal structure for growth.

Debt Structuring

Optimizing capital structure through syndicated loans, corporate bonds, and restructuring existing debt obligations.

Risk Advisory

Managing financial risks, including currency exposure, interest rate volatility, and regulatory compliance.
