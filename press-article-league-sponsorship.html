Umelusi Extends League Sponsorship Deal

Sports • 2025-02-20

  1. Home
  2. About Us
  3. Hierarchy
← Back to Pressroom

Umelusi Group has extended its headline sponsorship of the Eswatini National Golf League for a third year. The partnership reflects the Group’s investment in community and excellence through sport.

“It’s a natural fit,” says Sifiso Mabuza. “The values of discipline, long-term strategy, and integrity are shared across both investing and sport.”
