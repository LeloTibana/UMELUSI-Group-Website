Investment Analyst

Mbabane, Eswatini • Posted: May 10, 2025

  1. Home

Role Overview

As an Investment Analyst at Umelusi Fund Managers, you'll support portfolio decisions with rigorous financial analysis, macroeconomic monitoring, and sector-specific research. This role is ideal for candidates with a sharp quantitative mind, a passion for African markets, and a commitment to long-term value creation.

Key Responsibilities

  • Conduct equity and fixed income research across Southern African markets
  • Develop investment theses and valuation models using Excel and Python
  • Support risk analysis, performance attribution, and asset allocation frameworks
  • Prepare briefing documents for the Investment Committee

Minimum Requirements

  • Bachelor’s degree in Finance, Economics, or related field
  • 2+ years experience in asset management, private equity, or financial consulting
  • Proficiency in Excel; familiarity with Python or R is a plus
  • Strong communication and presentation skills
