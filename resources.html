Frequently Asked Questions

Investment Management focuses specifically on managing assets (stocks, bonds, funds) to meet growth objectives. Wealth Management is a holistic service that encompasses investment management along with financial planning, tax services, estate planning, and retirement strategies. Umelusi Fund Managers offers both integrated services.

Umelusi FinServe is an authorized agent for **Western Union Services**, allowing you to send or receive money to over 200 countries and territories globally. We also offer competitive foreign exchange rates for larger personal and business transfers.

Umelusi Capital specializes in **Corporate Finance** and **Investment Banking**, primarily serving established small-to-mid-market enterprises (SMEs) across Africa. We assist with mergers & acquisitions (M&A), raising capital, debt structuring, and private equity placement.

We are always looking for talented professionals to join our diverse team across all our brands (Fund Managers, Partners, Capital, and FinServe). Please visit our dedicated Career Opportunities page for the latest job listings and application process.
