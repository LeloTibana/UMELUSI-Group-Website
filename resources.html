Frequently Asked Questions

Umelusi Group is a diversified financial services holding company. Our primary focus is strategic financial growth, encompassing Investment Management (Fund Managers), Corporate Finance (Capital), Strategic Consulting (Partners), and Foreign Exchange/Money Transfer services (FinServ).

Getting started is easy. We recommend scheduling an initial consultation with one of our wealth advisors through the 'Contact' page. They will assess your financial goals, risk tolerance, and time horizon to recommend a suitable investment strategy and fund allocation.

Minimum investment thresholds vary depending on the specific product (e.g., collective investment schemes vs. bespoke mandates). For detailed information tailored to your investment class, please consult the Umelusi Fund Managers section or contact us directly.

Umelusi Capital works with mid-to-large corporate entities, institutional clients, and governments across Africa, specializing in mergers & acquisitions (M&A), debt structuring, equity placement, and strategic capital raising for major projects.

Umelusi Partners provides management consulting focused on strategy execution, digital transformation, operational restructuring, and organizational design. We work closely with executive teams to identify bottlenecks and implement sustainable performance improvements.

Yes, Umelusi FinServ offers comprehensive foreign exchange services, including spot transactions, forward contracts, and currency risk management solutions tailored to the needs of both corporate and individual clients engaging in international trade or remittance.

Our flagship reports (like the Global Market Outlook) are typically published quarterly or annually. Shorter market updates, economic commentaries, and sector-specific whitepapers are released on a rolling basis, often weekly or monthly, depending on market volatility and developments.

We employ a holistic and conservative approach to risk management, which includes quantitative analysis, regulatory compliance adherence, and rigorous stress testing across all our business units—from investment portfolios to corporate financing activities. Client asset security is our paramount concern.
