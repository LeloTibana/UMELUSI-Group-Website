Our Board of Directors

Strategic leadership guiding the future of Umelusi Group.

Thabo Magagula

Group Director

Mr. Magagula is the visionary leader and Group Director of Umelusi Group. With over two decades of experience in African financial markets, his expertise spans strategic planning, capital allocation, and risk management. He ensures Umelusi Group maintains its commitment to operational excellence and client-centric growth.

Under his direction, Umelusi Group has expanded its footprint across diverse financial sectors, establishing itself as a trusted partner in investment management, corporate finance, and wealth preservation. His deep understanding of the regulatory landscape and dedication to ethical governance form the foundation of the Group's success.
