Our Board of Directors

Strategic leadership guiding the future of Umelusi Group.

Meet Our Leadership

Our Board of Directors combines decades of financial expertise with strategic vision, guiding Umelusi Group's commitment to excellence and sustainable growth.

Thabo Magagula

Group Director

Mr. Magagula is the visionary leader and Group Director of Umelusi Group. With over two decades of experience in African financial markets, his expertise spans strategic planning, capital allocation, and risk management.

He ensures Umelusi Group maintains its commitment to operational excellence and client-centric growth. Under his direction, Umelusi Group has expanded its footprint across diverse financial sectors, establishing itself as a trusted partner in investment management, corporate finance, and wealth preservation.

His deep understanding of the regulatory landscape and dedication to ethical governance form the foundation of the Group's success.

Lomkhosi Magagula

Non-Executive Director

Ms. Magagula brings extensive corporate governance and oversight experience to the Board as a Non-Executive Director. Her background in law and strategic compliance provides invaluable guidance, ensuring Umelusi Group operates with the highest standards of integrity and transparency.

She plays a critical role in evaluating the Group's risk framework and contributing to the development of sustainable, long-term corporate strategy.

Her commitment is to ensure that the Group's mission to foster prosperity is achieved through sound financial principles and rigorous ethical practice.
