Strategic Asset Management and Wealth Creation

Umelusi Fund Managers is the dedicated investment and asset management arm of Umelusi Group. We deliver specialized investment strategies and wealth planning services designed to preserve and grow institutional capital and high-net-worth portfolios across Africa.

We partner with pension funds, corporate clients, and private individuals, focusing on long-term value creation through disciplined research and active portfolio management. Our expertise spans fixed income, equity, and alternative investment classes, tailored to meet complex fiduciary duties and specific client risk profiles.

Umelusi Business Golf League

Umelusi Fund Managers is proud to be the official sponsor of the **Business Golf League** at the prestigious **Mbabane Golf Club**. This monthly event is a key networking fixture for the Eswatini business community, providing a relaxed, professional setting for our clients and partners to connect. The league is held every **second Wednesday of the month** and brings together leading executives and decision-makers for competitive play and relationship building, reinforcing our commitment to community engagement and professional growth beyond traditional finance.
